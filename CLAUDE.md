# Product Overview

## Vision

This product helps people take better selfies by turning pose coaching into a live, intuitive camera experience.  
Instead of guessing how to stand, users see a clear pose guide directly on camera and can adjust in real time.

The core promise is simple: **open camera, pick a pose, align quickly, get a better photo result**.

## Problem We Solve

Most users struggle with:
- Not knowing how to pose naturally.
- Looking awkward in selfies without guidance.
- Spending too much time retaking photos.

Our app solves this by giving visual direction before the photo is taken, not after.

## Product Experience

The app is a mobile-first camera interface with three main pieces:
- **Live camera view** where the user sees themselves.
- **Pose guide overlay** that shows how to move.
- **Pose gallery** that helps users browse and choose pose ideas quickly.

The experience is designed to feel lightweight, fast, and confidence-building.

## Target User

People who want better selfies for:
- Social media posts
- Profile photos
- Casual personal photos
- Everyday moments where they want to look more polished

## Core User Flow

1. User opens the app camera.
2. User taps **Generate Poses** to create fresh pose ideas from the current selfie frame.
3. App shows a gallery of pose options.
4. User selects a pose from the gallery.
5. Live overlay guides the user into alignment.
6. User adjusts posture and framing until pose looks right.
7. User proceeds to capture/share workflow.

## Experience Principles

- **Guidance over complexity**: clear visual instructions, minimal cognitive load.
- **Fast feedback**: users should understand what to do in seconds.
- **Confidence-first**: reduce awkwardness and decision fatigue.
- **Mobile-native feel**: camera-first interactions and smooth, modern UI.

## Business Value

- Increases successful selfie outcomes in fewer attempts.
- Creates a distinctive AI-assisted camera experience.
- Provides a strong demo story for hackathon judging: practical AI that improves a real daily behavior.
- Enables future expansion into personalized pose packs, social sharing, and premium creative modes.

## Scope Direction

The immediate goal is an MVP that proves:
- Users can generate pose ideas quickly.
- Users can follow live guidance without confusion.
- The app meaningfully improves pose quality in a single session.

Later iterations can expand personalization, style packs, and advanced creative controls.
